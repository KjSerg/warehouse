Our warehouse is your reliable partner in the storage and handling of goods

Our warehouse is a modern logistics center designed to meet the needs of our customers. From the very beginning, our mission has been to provide quality storage and handling services that meet the highest standards. The company was founded by a group of like-minded people who wanted to create a place where every customer could find effective solutions for their business development. We started with a small warehouse, but over the years we have become one of the leaders in the logistics industry

Pricing
Service Fees

We offer a transparent pricing system for storage and processing services. The cost of short-term storage is $0.60 per cubic foot per month, with the first 15 days free. Long-term storage is $0.50 per cubic foot per month for leases of 3 months or more. Additional services, such as repacking, labeling, or forklift use, are available at competitive rates. For more detailed information, please refer to our price list.
